About the App

Built for the one thing textbooks skip

  • Solve the irregular-reading problem Japanese numbers change sound depending on context. JP Numbers Drill focuses specifically on those patterns — ぷん vs ふん, よじ vs よんじ, ついたち, はつか — with notes explaining why.
  • For anyone studying Japanese Beginners building foundations, JLPT N5–N3 students, or advanced learners who want to eliminate hesitation when encountering numbers in the wild.
  • 5 minutes a day is enough Short, focused sessions fit into commutes or breaks. The spaced-repetition logic ensures your time is spent on what actually needs work.
